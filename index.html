Boneless Bangus

Step 1: Make crispy garlic and garlic oil

Ingredients needed: Oil and Garlic

Step 2: fry your bangus

Ingredients needed: Salt, pepper, garlic oil

Step 3: Make bangus sauce

Ingredients needed: Garlic oil, onions, soy sauce,

calamansi juice, oyster sauce, brown sugar, and water

Step 4: Serve

Ingredients

1 cup neutral oil

8 cloves garlic, minced

2 small or 1 large unmarinated boneless bangus

1 small red onion, minced

¼ cup soy sauce

3 tbsp oyster sauce

3 tbsp calamansi juice

2 tbsp brown sugar

¼ cup water

1 tbsp cornstarch, dissolved in 1 tbsp water
